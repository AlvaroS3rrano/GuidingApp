npx expo run:android --device

You will need to update your server’s IP address in three places:

1. Update the IP in Android’s XML:
   - Open android/app/src/main/res/xml/network_security_config.xml
   - Replace
     <domain includeSubdomains="true">YOUR.SERVER.IP.OR.HOSTNAME</domain>
     with your development server IP, e.g.
     <domain includeSubdomains="true">192.168.1.25</domain>

2. Start ngrok:
   - In a terminal, run:
     ngrok http https://localhost:8443 --host-header="localhost:8443"
   - Look for the “Forwarding” line, for example:
     https://abcd1234.ngrok-free.app -> https://localhost:8443

3. Copy the ngrok host into your constant:
   - From the forwarding URL, take the part between “https://” and “/”, e.g.
     abcd1234.ngrok-free.app
   - In app/constants/consts.ts set:
     export const COMP_IP = 'abcd1234.ngrok-free.app';
